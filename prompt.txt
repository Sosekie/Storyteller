As the sun dipped into the horizon, Mickey Mouse's hearty meal was savored by the townsfolk, who'd never seen a mouse devour a roast chicken with such gusto.

The aroma of roasting poultry wafted through the streets, enticing the residents to gather 'round and sample the tasty treat.

Little did they know, Mickey's love for roast chicken would be the catalyst for a legendary feast that would bring the whole town together in a grand celebration.
